Derby Evening Telegraph:

Cross o'th Hands, a driving folk band who take their name from a Derbyshire village, have just released their debut album "HANDMADE" which has a variety of rhythms and moods and highlights the musical skills of the performers..........The new album contains some tasteful instrumentals as well as tuneful songs and is definitely well worth a listen.
